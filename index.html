Paper Trails & Tales Book Club

Creating Connection Through Stories

In a world where we're all craving genuine connection, I wanted to bridge that gap through our shared love of reading. The joy of discovering a great book shouldn't be a solitary experience, so I created a space where book lovers can finally rave about their latest literary finds without judgment!

My approach is intentionally different: no assigned readings, no pressure. Everyone brings their favorite reads of the month, and we spend hours sharing stories, discovering new genres, and making real connections. Every meeting is held at a new location across Boston‚Äîfrom cozy independent bookstores and stunning libraries to scenic parks and trendy cafes. We explore the city while exploring literature!

Each gathering has its own theme and activities, creating those pure girlhood moments we all crave. Think book-themed picnics, paintings, author event outings, or simply finding the perfect cafe in a new neighborhood. It's part book club, part Boston adventure, part community celebration.

What started as casual conversations with friends about books has become a passion project teaching me everything about community building, event planning, and creative leadership. Turns out, when you give people permission to be excited about what they love while exploring an amazing city together, great things happen.
